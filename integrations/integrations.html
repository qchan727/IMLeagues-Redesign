Integrations

IMLeagues is able to interact with various systems

SINGLE SIGN ON (SSO)

Enabling Single Sign On (SSO) allows participants to login directly through the campus portal that they are already familiar with, using their current username and password. First time users will have their IMLeagues account created on the fly using the information securely passed from your portal, no registration required

PAYMENT

You can use our default PayPal integration simply by selecting Accept Online Payments in League Settings, or link payments to your payment processor of choice. All sensitive information is collected directly by the 3rd party processor, ensuring your transactions are safe and secure
